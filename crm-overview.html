Before This System Existed

Every growing pool business hits the same wall. The tools that worked at 10 clients collapse completely at 50.

📋

Leads Were Getting Lost

Enquiries saved in personal phones and notebooks. Follow-ups forgotten when the team was busy on site.
Every lead is saved instantly. The system sends reminders automatically. Nothing slips through.
🏗️

No Visibility Into Projects

Checking project status meant calling the supervisor. Payment status meant checking personal WhatsApp.
Every project has a live page. Payments, progress, photos — all visible without calling anyone.

Money Moving With No Record

Payments came via cash, UPI, and bank. Expenses paid from personal pocket. No idea if a project was profitable.
Every rupee in and out is logged per project. Profit/loss of every site is visible in real time.
🧾

Invoices Were Untracked

Invoices made in Word/Excel, formatted differently each time, shared on WhatsApp, never stored properly.
GST invoices generated in seconds, saved automatically, linked to the client account permanently.

AMC Contracts Neglected

AMC service visits were missed. Contracts were not renewed. Clients quietly switched to competitors.
Service schedule tracked per client. Visit history maintained. Renewals never missed.

Documents Impossible to Find

Designs, quotations, and invoices spread across 6 different phones in WhatsApp chats.
Every document stored against the client. Anything found in under 10 seconds.
👤

No Staff Accountability

No record of who called whom, who visited which site, or who approved a purchase.
Every action logged with a name and timestamp. Full audit trail always available.
🧠

Knowledge Walked Out the Door

When a team member left, their client contacts, project notes, and history left with them.
All knowledge lives in the system, not in anyone's phone or memory.

The Core Problem

A pool construction business is complex — multiple ongoing projects, AMC contracts, supplier relationships, staff coordination, and client communication all happening simultaneously. Managing all of this through WhatsApp, notebooks, and gut feel is not a strategy. It is a liability. Every missed follow-up, untracked payment, and lost document is money left on the table.

Features — Sales & Operations
What the System Does

26 features built specifically for a pool construction and AMC business.

🔐

Role-Based Access Control

CEO, Admin, Partner, and Customer Support roles. Each person sees only what they need. Sensitive financial data stays protected.

📊

Business Dashboard

Live KPIs — active leads, revenue collected, follow-ups due, projects in progress. Complete business overview in one screen.

🏊

Construction Leads

Full pipeline for pool construction enquiries — track status from first call through to won or lost, with notes and history.

🔧

AMC Leads

Separate pipeline for Annual Maintenance Contract enquiries with AMC-specific fields and renewal tracking.

📌

Kanban Pipeline

Visual drag-and-drop board showing all leads across every stage simultaneously. Instantly see where deals are stuck.

🔔

Follow-Up Tracker

Scheduled reminders per lead with due dates and call notes. Dashboard badge shows overdue follow-ups so nothing is forgotten.

📞

Call Tracker

Log every call with outcome and notes. Filter by staff member. Full call history per client always available.

🎨

Design Module

Upload and store pool design files linked to specific clients. Version tracking. Professional client-ready view mode.

📄

Quotation Module

Generate and store itemised quotations per lead. Track sent, approved, and rejected status with full history.

📤

Send to Client

Professional document-sending workflow. Select files and share a clean link. Track what was sent to whom and when.

⭐

Reviews & Feedback

Collect and store client reviews and site feedback with star ratings. All reviews in one place across all platforms.

💬

WhatsApp Greeting Generator

Generate personalised festival and occasion greetings for bulk WhatsApp sending to all clients at once.

Features — Finance & Operations
🏗️

Construction Site Tracker

Milestone progress, site photos, material delivery logs, supervisor visit logs. Full structured timeline per active project.

🏠

AMC Site Tracker

Schedule and log AMC visits — chemicals used, equipment serviced, issues found, next visit date. Complete service history per pool.

🛒

Procurement Management

Purchase orders, supplier tracking, item-wise procurement logs linked to specific sites. Know what is ordered, received, and pending.

🏭

Vendor Directory

Centralised supplier contacts with categories, payment terms, and notes. Never lose a vendor contact when a team member leaves.

📦

Inventory Management

Track stock levels, minimum thresholds, and usage per site. Eliminate duplicate purchasing and stock-outs.

💼

M2A Site Accounts

Full project financial ledger for M2A sites — payments in, expenses out, running balance, category-wise expense tracking.

🏦

Elite Pool Site Accounts

Same financial ledger for EP projects — construction and AMC tracked separately per site with live profit/loss balance.

📈

Account Detail View

Deep-dive per site — full transaction log with running balance, uploaded invoices, and CRM-generated invoices all in one place.

🧾

GST Invoice Generator

A4-compliant tax invoices with dynamic line items, CGST/SGST/IGST toggle, amount in words. PDF download and direct print. Auto-logged and auto-deducted from site account.

📁

Invoice Management (Per Site)

Upload external invoices (PDF/image) and view CRM-generated invoices per site. Individual download, bulk download, delete with full history.

🏢

Office Expenses

Track company-level costs — salaries, rent, utilities, petty cash. Category filters and monthly summaries.

👥

Staff & Attendance

Manage user accounts and roles. Log daily attendance — present, absent, half day, leave. Monthly summaries per employee.

🔒

Backup & Restore

One-click export of the entire database as a JSON file. Upload to restore. Factory reset with confirmation. Full backup history log.
